Frequently asked

How do I create an account?

Click “Log in” on the home page, then “Create an account.” You'll need an email and a password — that's it to get started.

Is Tester.io self-custody?

Yes. Your keys and tokens stay yours; we provide the tools to trade and monitor without taking custody of your assets.

How does the token sale work?

Contributions are secured on-chain. See the token metrics page for supply and distribution details.

Where are the API docs?

Everything for developers lives in the docs — authentication, market data, and orders.

How do I reset my password?

On the login screen, choose “Forgot password?” and follow the email instructions. Still stuck? Email support@tester.io.
