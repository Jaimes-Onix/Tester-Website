Risk Disclosures.

Last updated: June 1, 2026

Trading crypto assets — especially with leverage — involves significant risk. Please read this carefully before you trade.

1Leverage risk

Leverage magnifies both gains and losses. You can lose more than your initial margin and positions may be liquidated automatically when margin requirements are not met.

2Market volatility

Crypto markets can move sharply and without warning. Prices may gap, and liquidity can disappear during periods of stress.

3No financial advice

Nothing on Tester.io is investment, legal, or tax advice. Figures and counters shown on the site may be illustrative. Do your own research and consider professional advice.

4Technology risk

  • Smart contracts may contain undiscovered vulnerabilities.
  • Network congestion can delay transactions.
  • You are responsible for securing your own keys.

5Regulatory risk

The legal status of crypto assets varies by jurisdiction and may change. Ensure your use of the platform complies with your local laws.
